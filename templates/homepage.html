Welcome to PyWeather Bot

PyWeather is weather bot created in Python and Flask and it works alongwith online weather APIs. It is made by Himanshu Sharma. It works in a simple way. You just need to tell it how you will input a city. Whether by its name, pincode, latitude or longitude. After ticking that, you will be taken to a new page where you can type that respective information and click submit. In no time the bot will return you the weather of that city. So don't wait, click any of the options below.

By City Name
By Pincode
By Geo Coordinates
